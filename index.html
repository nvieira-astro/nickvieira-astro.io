About me

I began my postdoc at the Center for Interdisciplinary Exploration and Research in Astrophysics (CIERA) at Northwestern University, in Evanston, Illinois, in October of 2025. I previously completed my PhD at McGill, in my hometown of Montreal, Quebec, Canada, in the Summer of 2025. I'm currently most interested in multi-messenger astrophysics and how to most efficiently perform it, and searching for the site where the heaviest elements in the Universe are made.

In my free time, I love to play bass guitar, read, swim, cook, and go to shows (especially hardcore punk / thrash metal!).
